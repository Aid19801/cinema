The Conjuring 2

Year: 2016     Runtime: 140mins     Subs: English
Certificate: 15     (Violence/Strong Horror)
In 1977, paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren come out of a self-imposed sabbatical to travel to Enfield where they meet Peggy Hodgson, an overwhelmed single mother of four who tells the couple that something evil is in her home. Ed and Lorraine believe her story when the youngest daughter starts to show signs of demonic possession. As the Warrens try to help the besieged girl, they become the next targets of the malicious spirit.

Horror sequel based on the infamous Enfield haunting, from James Wan.


Starring: Vera Farmiga, Frances O'Connor, Patrick McAuley, Lauren Esposito, Benjamin Haigh, Madison Wolfe, Simon McBurney and Patrick Wilson
